About us

Skillis-Innov8 is a mobile application aimed at transforming the Students Industrial Work Experience Scheme (SIWES) by digitally connecting students with industrial training opportunities. It streamlines the placement process and fosters a more efficient and accessible ecosystem for industrial training.
